D-CDP is the Customer Data Platform for on-premises deployments.
There are 5 main functions of D-CDP
  1. Provide a customer analytics solution for data-driven organizations
  2. Unification to Single-view Profile: Consistent & Unified 360 degree Customer View
  3. Segmentation with Visual Data Query Technology
  4. Personalization by Open Source Recommender System
  5. Activation with open Data Services
